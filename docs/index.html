About

About Prophet Givens

Driven by the very things that move the heart of God, Prophet Givens Chizenga carries a powerful Prophetic mantle upon his life, illuminating the path of righteousness for believers around the world.

I am Prophet Givens Chizenga, and I am delighted to share my journey of faith with you. This website is a glimpse into my life as a believer, prophet, and minister of God's Word. From an early age, I heard the voice of God calling me to be a vessel for His glory and a messenger of hope and salvation. With unwavering faith and a deep conviction in the power of the Gospel, I have dedicated my life to proclaiming God's truths and guiding others on their spiritual journey.

As a believer, I stand firm in my trust in God and His Word. Through His grace, I have witnessed countless lives transformed and restored. My desire is to inspire and encourage you to deepen your relationship with God, walk in obedience, and discover the abundant life found in Him. I have been blessed with insights and revelations from God. It is my joy to share these divine messages with you, unveiling the mysteries of His Word and helping you navigate the challenges of life with faith and strength.

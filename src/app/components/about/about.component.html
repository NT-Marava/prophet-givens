Blessings,

I am Prophet Givens Chizenga, and I'm delighted that you've joined me here today. I am a Minister of the Word of God, and my unwavering passion lies in sharing the unlimited power of Christ Jesus. With a profound Prophetic mantle upon my life, I strive to illuminate the path of righteousness for believers worldwide. On this website, you will find enlightening teachings, experience the transformative power of the Gospel, and be inspired in your faith journey.

Born and raised in the vibrant city of Chitungwiza, Zimbabwe, my spiritual journey began at a remarkably early age when I received a divine calling to be a worker of the Kingdom of Heaven. This calling ignited a deep passion within me to spread the Gospel and bring hope, healing, and salvation to the lives of people around me.

“ Cheer up Believer. The Word Works! ”

In 2016, I embraced another significant milestone in my life as I joined in holy matrimony with my beloved wife, Yollunda Chizenga. Together, we embarked on an adventure of love, faith, and shared purpose. Following our marriage, Yollunda and I relocated to the city of Cape Town, South Africa, where I dedicated myself to the relentless ministry of preaching the Gospel. Fueled by an unquenchable hunger to guide my generation towards a deeper relationship with God, I witnessed the transformative power of faith and the impact it had on people's lives. Inspired by these experiences, I realized that God was calling me to establish a Ministry that would serve as a beacon of light and hope for individuals seeking spiritual guidance.

Gathering Of Believers International Church

In February 2018, Gathering Of Believers Int Ministry was born, with Cape Town as its humble foundation. Led by my vision of soul-winning, preaching the Gospel to all nations, and restoring hope to God's people, the Ministry began its journey. Through dynamic worship services, impactful conferences, and various outreach programs, Gathering Of Believers Int Ministry strives to equip believers with the tools they need to walk in victory and fulfill their God-given destinies.

With a deep understanding of the Word of God and a profound Prophetic anointing, I am committed to bringing hope, healing, and transformation to individuals seeking a closer relationship with God. Through my ministry, I aim to make the Word of God accessible and applicable to everyday life, helping believers overcome challenges and experience the abundant life that God desires for them.
